Our Story

Born from the Soul of Hampi

Laughing Buddha Restaurant was created with one clear idea: serve honest food in a peaceful environment. Inspired by the slow, soulful vibe of Hampi, our restaurant is a place where you can sit back, breathe easy, and enjoy every bite without rush or noise.

We believe food tastes better when it is fresh, thoughtfully prepared, and enjoyed in a relaxed space.
